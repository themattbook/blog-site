My name is Matt. I'm a FullStack Engineer and 3-time olympic gold medalist, accomplished physicist, and I wear velcro shoes...

...and only one of those statements are true.

It took me 3 hours to think of something noteworthy to say here. Literally. After several long deliberations with my inner monologue, I decided to let you in on the struggle. No, not very professional, but also... personable.

I'm 41. I write code. I hate it when people ask for "pixel-perfect" and then expect "responsive". I often forget the small details. I am terrible at commenting my code. I don't like large teams. I bite my nails. I think TypeScript was invented by people who wanted to milk the budget on their project as much as possible. Either that, or they've never heard of console logging. Mobile-first is the worst idea humans have ever had.

I'm obsessed with white space and minimal designs. I love React, I have a love-hate relationship with Vue, Android > iPhone, and PC gaming is the only way. I once loved Tailwindcss until I met Sass. Clean code is subjective. I don't eat pork. Writing music is therapy. I'm in school earning my Master's Degree in Psychology.
